Welcome to CarPool!

CarPool makes ridesharing easier by allowing you to instanty pay or request money from your friends for shared rides.

CarPool automatically calculates the amount of money that was spent on gas for the ride and you can just Venmo your friends right from the app. You can also split it amongst other people who shared the ride too, effectively eliminating all the fuss about gas money!
